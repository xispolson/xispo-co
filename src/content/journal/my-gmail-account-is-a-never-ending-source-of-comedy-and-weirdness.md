One reason is that google requires at least six characters in the username, which means I can&#39;t have "xispo" as an option. Boo. Another is that it&#39;s a pain in the ass to change e-mails. So many accounts are tied to my e-mail and the thought of doing double duty for x number of months to ease the transition irks me. The last is that my google account gets a constant stream of spam. There&#39;s the usual V1@grA stuff, but I also get a decent amount of e-mails that are meant for someone else. Some of them are pretty&#0160;personal, but not *that* personal, heh. My initial reaction to getting these was to send a polite e-mail to the sender, letting them know they had the wrong person. Some replied and stopped e-mailing my account. Others didn&#39;t.&#0160;
