Was a treat to see Kristin and co play on Friday. I could tell right away
that David is no longer on the skins (bummer), but her quiet intensity is
still there! Also cool to see the band has morphed into a family affair
with son on bass and partner on drums.

She played new material, mostly, which lead me to try to listen to some of
my faves over the weekend. I was astonished to discover that there's a
signifcant chunk of back catalog not available online. Their first album,
for example, is not on any streaming, nor is their album Limbo. Guessing
this has to do with label/publishing weirdness, but still bummed that more
folks don't have access to more of their catalog. You can listen to their
<a href="
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E1Xd2jY3zWQ&list=RDE1Xd2jY3zWQ&start_radio=1"
target="_blank">first record on YouTube</a>, though.
