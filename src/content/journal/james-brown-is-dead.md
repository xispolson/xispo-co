During the holiday visit to sis and niece, I made a trek up to NYC and happened to arrive the same day James Brown&#39;s body was on display at the Apollo. So I hopped on the A train from Penn Station, trekked up to Harlem and stood "on line" for longer than I care to admit. The crowd was pretty fun though, which made the wait in the cold less painful. The godfather looked very peaceful and funky, decked out in a blue suit with rhinestone trim. No pics were allowed inside, unfortunately.
