Chris and Ethan are joined by veteran games journalist Chris “CJ” Johnston for a deep dive into Nintendo’s latest handheld, the Switch 2. Drawing on CJ’s 30 years in the industry,including his influential time at Electronic Gaming Monthly, the conversation blends first impressions of the Switch 2 with a broader historical perspective. From launch games and peripherals to the joy of playing Yakuza on the go, this isn’t your standard hardware review, but a thoughtful look at how portable gaming continues to evolve.
